# Phase 9 — Manuscript

## Phase objective

Write, polish, and submit the flagship manuscript. Structure: a brief
introduction framing the science-of-science gap, methods, the three
findings plus the open-source contribution, and discussion of implications
for science policy, journal editors, and field self-monitoring. Internal
review by all co-authors, followed by external review from 2–3 trusted
senior colleagues including any engaged science-of-science collaborator.
Simultaneous bioRxiv preprint with submission. Target venue: *Nature
Communications* first (broad audience, accepts methods + findings papers,
science-of-science fits), with backups *Nature Human Behaviour*, *Nature
Machine Intelligence*, or *Patterns* (Cell Press, open access, faster
decisions). Deliverables: submitted manuscript, preprint with DOI, cover
letter, and suggested-reviewers list.
