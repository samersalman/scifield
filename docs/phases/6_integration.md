# Phase 6 — Integration

## Phase objective

Combine the four axes (thematic, epistemic, novelty, forecasting) into the
three target findings and generate the analyses and figures that drive the
manuscript. **F1 (epistemic cascade):** for each topic, plot evidence-quality
and topic-volume trajectories and test the lead-lag relationship with
cross-correlation or Granger causality. **F2 (dual-novelty divergence):**
cross-tabulate semantic and structural novelty, trend archetype
distributions over time, and relate archetypes to downstream citation
impact. **F3 (predictive validity):** frame the Phase 5 forecasting result
within the broader narrative. Bonus analyses include cross-journal seeding
networks, per-specialty differences, and institutional/geographic patterns.
Deliverable: a draft results section with publication-quality figures, a
preprint-ready figure set, and a fully reproducible analyst notebook.
Success criterion: at least two of the three target findings hold with
appropriate statistical support and co-authors agree the narrative is
coherent.
